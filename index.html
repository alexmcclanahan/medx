Empowering healthcare professionals to do what they do best
Let us root through health insurance coverage policies while you, the healthcare professional, do all of the patient care. Our service will deliver to you the precise ICD-10 codes that an insurance company needs to see in order to cover a specialty treatment. All that you have to do is enter the necessary insurance and treatment data above, and our algorithm will do all the work.
Our philosophy
Our core philosophy underlying this technology is that the act of diagnosing and caring for your patients should be as enjoyable as possible. Prescribing a specialty medication should no longer be a stressful, ambiguous process for healthcare professionals in today's information age. By extention, yours patients should not have to worry that their treatments and medications will be denied by their insurance company. Let us speed your office efficiency by ending the 'back and forth' haggle with health insurance companies.

Our mission

Our mission is to empower healthcare professionals to do what they do best. As a healthcare professional, you have likely experienced the frustration that comes with a health insurance company refusing to pay for your patient's specialty medication because your diagnosis codes, upon billing, failed to match the diagnosis codes outlined in their coverage policy -- thereby rendering that medication 'not covered.' We believe in putting the power back in the hands of dedicated healthcare professionals like yourself, literally. By entering a patient's insurance company, their plan (if needed), and the treatment you desire for them, the precise ICD-10 diagnosis codes constituting medical necessity for coverage (as explicitly outlined in their coverage policy) will be delivered directly to you. This gives you the power to make the most effective decisions regarding your patient's health and plan of treatment once again.
